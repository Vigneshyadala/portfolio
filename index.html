I build systems that plug AI into real problems.

Software engineer and AI engineer based in Chennai, currently completing a B.Tech in Computer Science at Bharath Institute of Science & Technology.

My work sits at the intersection of full-stack development and applied AI — taking models like Gemini, Claude, and PyTorch and wiring them into systems people can actually use: a civic-education assistant, an SME financial analyzer, a job-matching bot, a species classifier.

Most of my projects start from a real friction point — a student losing marksheets, an SME owner unable to read a balance sheet, a job seeker drowning in tabs — and end as a deployed, working product, not just a notebook.

I'm comfortable owning a project end-to-end: frontend, backend, AI integration, deployment, and the unglamorous parts like security rules and input sanitization.
